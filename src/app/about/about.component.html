When I was 11 years old, my mother passed away from breast cancer. Nearly a decade later, a psychologist diagnosed me with Post Traumatic Stress Disorder and generalized anxiety. The new way of life that I adapted to was symptomatic of these disorders, rather than something I viewed as normal.

I subsequently began EMDR therapy. The lack of access to mental health collaborate frusturates me, so I have made it my mission to create accessible therapy for everyone who suffers from PTSD and anxiety disorders. We are a team of scientist, business leaders, and software engineers.
